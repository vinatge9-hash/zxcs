Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our dog care services and policies.

How do you handle pickups and drop-offs?

We coordinate times with you via text updates. Our drivers use secure locking mechanisms and sign-in/out procedures.

What if my dog is shy or reactive?

We tailor introductions, create calm zones, and use positive reinforcement to build comfort.

Are meals or meds provided?

We can follow your feeding schedule and administer medications as directed with prior authorization.
